An OKR management tool to keep your team on track

Okra is a fresh approach to team management.

You have deadlines. Tasks have different priorities. Moving a task from one column to the next doesn't reflect the nature of your work. Okra does. A hierarchical tree structure allows you to think about your organization in terms of priority. For any given timeframe (quarterly, annual...) create a tree to keep your team on the same page.

Trasparency has never been so easy

  1. Start by creating a tree.
  2. Then create an objective.
  3. Next, add a key result that defines, quantifiably, what it means to meet that objective.
  4. Add a task to each key result.

You can assign members of your team to any node in the tree.
